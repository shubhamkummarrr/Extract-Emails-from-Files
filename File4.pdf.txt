If anyone needed documentation from that project, they could probably request it from the engineering coordinator whose email happened to be rahul.mehta.projects@energysmart.co.

While reviewing the printed documents, a junior officer pointed out a handwritten note near the bottom of the page that simply read 9012345679 — likely the mobile number of a field technician who had supervised the earlier installation.
